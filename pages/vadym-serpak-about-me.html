About Me

Hello, I am Ben. I'm an aspiring data scientist who is insatiatiably curious about the field and passionate about moving the future forward.

I used to be a student at Metis where I graduated from the Data Science Immersive program in April of 2016. Before that, I was a world-class professional poker player. Awhile back, I co-founded an instructional video site now iveyleague.com teaching people how to win at poker which sold to Phil Ivey. Solving extremely difficult problems using probability and statistics brings me joy.

I am a data scientist transitioning from playing poker professionally. I am mathematically inclined and obviously extremely familar with probability and statistics. I enjoy the process of solving complex problems which involve uncertainty.
